Service

Our creative team understands what "does" or doesn't" make a website pop. Through years of actually building websites, we know in order to stay competitive, your website should really look amazing.


One of the web design standards that our team embraced many years ago, was building responsive websites. By attending educational training seminars and doing research on a regular ongoing basis, our team reviews new techniques and styles in website design that are at the forefront. We consider, among other things, the following when it comes to website design:

  • Typography
  • Large and Responsive Images
  • Clear Calls to Action
  • Engaging Videos
  • Semi-Flat Design
  • Clear and Polished Website Navigation
  • Product or Feature Videos
  • Slider (Featuring Images and/or Youtube Videos)
  • Mobile Design
  • Full Screen
